Circular Arc Airfoil Data¶

Many indoor models use circular arc airfoils. In order to conduct aerodynamic studies of these models, we need standard coefficient data. Unfortunately, research into flight in the range of our indoor models is pretty scarce. However, recent interest in micro UAV vehicles has led to some recent research that is solving this deficiency in data.

I found data for 3% and 5% circular arc airfoils, but that data is not totally applicable for our flights. It will serve as a basis for this study, at least until I find better data.

Using the procedure discussed earlier for digitizing data, we will look at the coefficient data I found.
